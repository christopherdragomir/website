About

I am a PhD student in Operations, Information, and Decisions at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. My research focuses on platform pricing, peer-to-peer marketplaces, and the design of electricity markets and EV charging incentives.

Before Wharton, I completed an MSE and a BSE in Operations Research and Financial Engineering at Princeton University, where I worked on autonomous vehicles, mobility equity, and transportation systems.
